game description
Spinball is a fast, fun, and competitive multi-player sport game with non-stop scoring action. Rack up the points as you spin your paddles to smack the ball towards the goal, and try not to accidentally score against your own team!
Imagine if Pong, air hockey, and foosball were combined into one amazing retro-inspired game. Well, you don't have to imagine, because Spinball is real! The mechanics may be simple, but the gameplay is captivating. Think quickly because spinning the wrong way can mean the difference between capturing the lead in a close game and dooming your team to a devastating defeat. Play with your friends, or challenge some of our highly-trained AI spinbots, like DINGUS, FARKLE, or STEVE. Compete for Spinball glory in our 9 state-of-the-art arenas. Keep your skills sharp with our single player challenge mode. Track your progress on the leaderboard to become the Spinball heavyweight champion of the world!
